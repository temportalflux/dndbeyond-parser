Gorthok the Thunder Boar

Gorthok the Thunder Boar
Huge Monstrosity, Chaotic Evil
Armor Class 15 (natural armor)
Hit Points 73 (7d12 + 28)
Speed 50 ft.
STR
20 (+5)
DEX
11 (+0)
CON
19 (+4)
INT
6 (-2)
WIS
10 (+0)
CHA
14 (+2)
Damage Resistances Bludgeoning, Piercing, and Slashing from Nonmagical Attacks
Damage Immunities Lightning, Thunder
Senses Darkvision 60 ft., Passive Perception 10
Languages --
Challenge 6 (2,300 XP)
Proficiency Bonus +3

Relentless (Recharges after a Short or Long Rest). If Gorthok takes 27 damage or less that would reduce it to 0 hit points, it is reduced to 1 hit point instead.

Actions

Multiattack. Gorthok makes two melee attacks: one with its lightning tusks and one with its thunder hooves.

Lightning Tusks. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 12 (2d6 + 5) slashing damage plus 7 (2d6) lightning damage.

Thunder Hooves. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 12 (2d6 + 5) bludgeoning damage plus 7 (2d6) thunder damage.

Lightning Bolt (Recharge 6). Gorthok shoots a bolt of lightning at one creature it can see within 120 feet of it. The target must make a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw, taking 18 (4d8) lightning damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.

Description

Gorthok is a primal nature spirit that takes the form of a boar as big as an elephant, with lightning that dances along its tusks. Gorthok serves the will of Talos, god of storms, and can be summoned during stormy weather to do the bidding of Talos‚Äôs evil followers. Like its patron deity, Gorthok revels in destruction.

Dragon of Icespire Peak

    I was going to give Gorthy an AC of 16 and Max HP out at 112, but am giving the party a group anchorites, orcs, orogs, various blights, and a few regular boars to fight as well. I ended up keeping AC and HP the same as the statblock here. Not a bad idea to increase them though. It's a boss fight!

    Legendary Actions

    Gorthok can take 1 legendary action, from the  two options below. Only 1 legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. Gorthok regains its spent legendary action at the start of its turn.

    1. Summon an electric boar within 30 feet which takes it turns on Gorthy's initiative. (regular boar stats, adding 1d4 or 1d6 lightning to it's attacks. Same resistances as Gorthok.) Gorthok can summon up to three, one per turn.
      Consider using a giant boar for the cost of two or all three if Gorthy is set at 112 HP (or base stat) and you want a tougher encounter. Probably increasing the lightning damage to 1d6 or 1d8 electric for them. Giant Boar standard HP is 42

    2. Reamplify (idk): Gorthok amps up and absorbs ions in the air (or magic energy, its d&d!) and gains 1d12 hp. Could be used to get up to 12 THP, but I wouldn't go beyond that. 
      When Gorthok uses this ability, it also adds 1d4 or 1d6 HP to the summoned electric boars if they are within a certain ranged based on dm discretion. (30 for me.)
