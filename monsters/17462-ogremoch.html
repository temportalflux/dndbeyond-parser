     Maximum rollable Hit points (180 HP). 

    Reading this stat block, it looks like Ogremoch's preferred strategy seems to be to protect itself and let minions do most of its fighting. Spawn earth elementals, then burrow underground so that only part of his body is exposed, giving three-quarters cover (+5 AC). The first part of the fight, He'd probably use his legendary action to spawn gargoyles (Which you might also consider giving Max HP, since it's a big HP investment for big boy) and lair actions to muddy or tremor his lair - his Gargoyles and elementals will have advantage on attacks against prone or restrained enemies. Since he doesn't earthglide like smaller earth elementals, I'd have him use his action to "move earth" to fill in any holes where too much of his body would be exposed.

    When he has around 250 HP left, I'd have him stop making Gargoyles and start attacking properly. Boulder against any targets that he already has advantage on attacks against, focusing on any targets that are "trapped in the earth" (Prone, restrained, anything that gives him attack advantage against them) focus on illuminating and stomping enemies. 

    When he gets desperate - less than 100 - Have him stone meld into a nearby rock face to escape the players, or just leave. Let him keep using his lair actions - Crystal spikes and earth tremors will continue to whittle down players while they try to figure out how to fight an actual cliff face.

    Have fun!
