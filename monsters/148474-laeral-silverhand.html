    I think the authors may have misnamed her Silver Fire as Spellfire.  

    While they are two versions of the same raw magical power they have different functions and meaning both in terms of gameplay and lore. Silver Fire is granted by Mystra to her Chosen to protect and sustain them, or unleashed by them as a fiery attack.  Spellfire is a rare random trait that any mortal could be born with that allows tvem to absorb magical attacks and transform that energy into healing or consuming fire.

     The 'Spellfire' ability here is consistent with the Silver Fire that Laeral and her sisters wielded in past editions and novels.
