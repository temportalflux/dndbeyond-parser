Actions

Multiattack. Bel makes three attacks: two with his greatsword and one with his tail.

Tail. Melee Weapon Attack: +16 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 25 (3d10 + 9) bludgeoning damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a DC 23 Constitution saving throw or be stunned until the end of its next turn.

     I disagree with this for a few reasons:

    1.) Meteor Swarm + 3 fireballs for the first round is 224 average damage, after that it would be 197 on average if he did only multiattack and 3 fireballs per round. His effective HP is actually 541 (364 + 30 (legendary actions) + 39 (fear aura. Normally thats 25% of effective HP if facing lv 10 players or lower, which you kinda can do in BG:DiA. I did 10% since it's a 1 round fear ability not 1 minute)  x 1.25 (damage immunities). The first round he is coming at you as a CR 26 creature. 

    2.) He is able to summon an ice devil (spear variant) one at a time (but no limit on how many during combat). You really should be accounting for the summoned creature's average damage output when calculating the CR as it's a special trait (dmg 278). If he summons an ice devil (spear variant) it raises his average damage per round by 46 (62 if you choose to summon regular ice devils). One summon raises that 197 damage to 243 on subsequent rounds (259 with regular ones).

    3.) With an effective HP of 541 and an effective AC of 23, his defensive CR is actually 26 not 24. If you account for the damage on just turn 1 with meteor and fireballs and no other turn his offensive CR is 26.

    4.) Using your same example of Bel casting meteor swarm, uses multiattack twice, summoning two ice devils, and 3 fireballs within 3 rounds. Bel uses meter swarm and then summons, then multiattack and summons, then multiattack and 3 fireballs. If you account for the summons' average damage, the average damage per round goes like this: (186, 205, 289) which is about 226 average damage between those 3 rounds. The big problem is that this damage increase with more summons has no cap, he can continue to summon ice devils with no limit on the damage output. Even in 3.5, in the book of vial deeds limited his summons to twice a day.
