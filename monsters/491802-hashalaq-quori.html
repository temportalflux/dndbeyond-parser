Description

Quori

Dal Quor is the plane of dreams and is currently dominated by a dark power known as il-Lashtavar, or the Dreaming Dark. Il-Lashtavar is served by a host of aberrations that are the embodiments of dreams and nightmares ‚Äî the quori. Because it is difficult for anything to physically travel to or from Dal Quor, quori in Eberron are typically encountered while possessing a host body. The Inspired are the most common type of willing host for the quori and are described earlier in this chapter.

Hashalaq Quori

Hashalaq quori are loremasters and judges, and are commonly known as dreamstealers. In their natural form, hashalaqs are composed of hundreds of translucent tendrils. They can compress and configure these tendrils to form a wide range of simple shapes. A point of blue light suspended within its tendrils serves as the hashalaq‚Äôs sensory organ, which can be moved around to suit the creature‚Äôs current shape.

The Power of Pleasure. Hashalaq quori have studied their mortal prey for centuries. Inhabiting human bodies as Inspired has granted the hashalaq a deep understanding of the hedonistic urges of humanity. As a result, they have developed numerous ways to control humanoids through pleasure ‚Äî even as they have developed a taste for such things themselves.

The Learned of Dal Quor. Hashalaq quori are the loremasters of Dal Quor. Even those hashalaqs who don‚Äôt make use of Inspired vessels dedicate themselves to studying Eberron and the planes, and they are among the few quori castes whose members understand the ways of magic. In Dal Quor, the hashalaq quori are typically found as advisors to the kalaraq. They also serve as judges, policing the quori and ensuring that the rivalries of the tsucora never threaten the greater plans of the Dreaming Dark.

    I don't see anything in the statblock that suggests the Quori cannot cast spells while possessing another creature especially given its spellcasting is completely innate with no components required. Using the "possessed target's statistics" does not preclude the Quori from accessing its Innate Spellcasting especially as Psionic spellcasting is a purely mental Intelligence-based endeavour that functions regardless of what body the mind is in. 
    To me the strategy intended is for the Quori to possess a humanoid then use Disguise Self, or any other spells, upon the body it is controlling.
